About Us

Pastor Tiffany Jones

Pastor Tiffany Phipps-Jones is a devoted mother, minister, and gifted singer who has been using her voice to glorify God since the age of five. Blessed with a powerful gift of song, she has shared her talents on numerous platforms, inspiring and uplifting many through music and ministry. Her journey of faith took a defining turn at the age of 16 when she gave her life to Christ. In 2014, she deepened her commitment to the call of God by attending Ministry School at Sons of Thunder and was ordained as a minister that same year. After years of dedicated service, she was ordained as a pastor in 2023, marking the next chapter of her spiritual leadership. In 2024, Pastor Tiffany Phipps-Jones was appointed as the Pastor of Assembly of the Saints of God, where she officially began leading services in 2025. Her passion for ministry is fueled by her love for God and a desire to see lives transformed. She continues to serve faithfully, sharing the gospel, encouraging believers, and using her voice—both in song and in word—to bring glory to the Kingdom of God.

Chief Apostle Carl Harris

Chief Apostle Carl Harris has dedicated his life to impacting generations with the power and truth of Jesus Christ. As the Senior Pastor at The Assembly of The Saints in Houston, Texas, since 2008, he has facilitated radical conversions, healings, and deliverances, transforming lives by challenging and empowering individuals through his ministry. He has also served as the Assistant Director of Royal Priesthood International Ministries, evangelizing communities and teaching Bible classes on leadership and spiritual warfare. In addition to his pastoral duties, he actively reaches out to the community through various outreach programs, including senior, homeless, and orphan ministries. He has a rich history of ministerial roles, spanning multiple churches and positions, where he has preached, taught Bible classes, and provided counseling and mentorship. Chief Apostle Harris' unwavering commitment to his vision and his extensive contributions to ministry have made a lasting impact on countless lives.

Apostle Janice Harris

Apostle Dr. Janice Harris has dedicated her life to impacting generations with the power and truth of Jesus Christ. As the Prophetess, Evangelist, Executive Director, and Treasurer at The Assembly of the Saints in Houston, Texas, since 2008, she has been instrumental in bringing radical conversions, healings, and deliverances to countless individuals. She actively evangelizes communities both locally and internationally, teaches Bible classes, and provides ministerial training. Dr. Harris is deeply committed to outreach programs, including senior, homeless, and orphan ministries, and mentors youths in various programs. Her leadership extends to overseeing church operations, managing finances, and organizing fundraising events. Additionally, she serves as the President of Royal Priesthood International Ministries, where she continues to evangelize and support the establishment of churches. Dr. Harris' unwavering dedication and extensive contributions to ministry have profoundly impacted the lives of many.

Chief Apostle Carl & Apostle Janice Harris (Overseers)

Chief Apostle Carl and Apostle Dr. Janice Harris, as overseers, have made significant contributions to the ministry, impacting countless lives with the power and truth of Jesus Christ. Their combined vision has led to radical conversions, healings, and deliverances, transforming individuals from powerless living to a spiritual life full of the gifts of the Spirit. They have been instrumental in evangelizing communities both locally and internationally, teaching Bible classes, and providing ministerial training. Their leadership extends to various outreach programs, including senior, homeless, and orphan ministries, and mentoring youths in multiple programs. They have also played a crucial role in overseeing church operations, managing finances, and organizing fundraising events to support their ministries. Their unwavering dedication and extensive accomplishments have profoundly influenced the lives of many, leaving a lasting legacy of faith and service.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be a light in the darkness, a beacon of hope in a world that is filled with despair. We believe that the church is called to be a place of refuge and healing for those who are hurting and broken. We are committed to sharing the love of Christ with all people, regardless of their background or circumstances. We believe that the church is a place where we can find forgiveness, healing, and restoration. We invite you to join us as we seek to be the hands and feet of Jesus in our community and beyond.
